March 29, 2021

10 minute read

Recently I came across an interesting issue. My University runs a field station at a rural property called Newholme where a grid of camera traps was set up a few years back. Cameras are solar-powered and form a mesh network where photos are relayed from one camera to another and finally to the base station located at the station building.
